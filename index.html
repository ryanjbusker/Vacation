💡 Good to Know

Quick Tips

🌤
Weather in June

Expect sunny 70–80°F days and cooler nights around 45–55°F. Afternoon thunderstorms are common — bring layers and a light rain jacket.

🧴
Altitude Awareness

Colorado Springs sits at 6,000+ feet. Drink extra water, apply sunscreen generously, and take it easy the first day if you're coming from sea level.

🎟
Book Ahead

Pikes Peak Cog Railway and Cave of the Winds sell out fast in peak season. Reserve tickets online before the trip.

🚗
Getting Around

Most attractions require a car. Monument is about 20 minutes north of downtown CS — build in drive time and coordinate carpools.
